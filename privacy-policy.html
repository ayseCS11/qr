Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

QR & Barcode Scanner ("the App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we may collect, how it is used, shared, and your rights regarding your data. By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to minimize personal data collection. Below are the categories of data the App may process:

2.1 Camera Access

  • The App requires access to your device camera to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • Camera data is processed locally in real-time for scanning purposes only.
  • No images or video are stored or transmitted to any server.

2.2 Scan History

  • The App maintains a history of scanned codes stored locally on your device.
  • You can view, search, filter, mark as favorite, or export history as CSV.
  • Scan history is never transmitted externally unless you explicitly share or export it.

2.3 Internet Access

  • Internet is used to verify scanned URLs for security, malware, or phishing.
  • Download advertisements or optional updates.
  • No personal information such as name, email, or phone number is transmitted automatically.

2.4 QR Code Generation

  • Generated QR codes (URLs, WiFi credentials, contacts, email, phone, text) are created locally.
  • Data is not sent to servers unless shared by the user.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to provide core functionality. These include:

  • Google ML Kit – For QR and barcode scanning.
  • Google AdMob – For displaying ads in the free version.
  • Other analytics or crash-reporting SDKs (if implemented).

These third-party services may collect limited technical or usage information. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Google AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google ML Kit privacy: https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

4. Advertising

Advertisements in the free version may collect non-personal data:

  • Device type, OS version, and app interactions
  • Advertising ID for personalized ads

Ad partners do not receive your personal data. You may opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

5. Analytics

The App may collect aggregated, anonymous usage data for performance improvements:

  • Number of scans, app launches, and feature usage
  • Crash reports for bug fixing

All analytics data is anonymous and does not personally identify users.

6. Data Retention and Security

  • Scan history is stored locally on your device until you delete it.
  • The App does not store personal information on servers.
  • Reasonable technical measures are used to secure local data against unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

You have rights regarding your data:

  • Access and view scan history stored locally
  • Delete or export scan history anytime
  • Opt out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Use the App offline to limit network data usage

If you wish to exercise your data rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us so it can be removed.

9. International Compliance

The App complies with applicable laws including GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California) for personal data handling. Users can:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of data
  • Opt out of any data collection for analytics or ads

Data requests should be submitted via the contact information below.
